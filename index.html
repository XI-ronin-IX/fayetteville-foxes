01 / Identity

Three words. Every shift.

Every decision on the ice runs through the same filter. If a play doesn't reflect one of these, it doesn't belong on a Foxes sheet.

01 / Brain

Cunning.

Read the play two passes ahead. Outthink the forecheck. A Foxes line controls tempo with position, not panic.

02 / Body

Ferocious.

First to the puck, last off the ice. We finish checks, we finish plays, and we finish the buzzer standing over the net.

03 / Clock

Unrelenting.

Sixty minutes of pressure. Down two in the third? We bring the intensity. The work rate is the identity.

02 / Next Puck Drop

Sunday morning. Wear orange.

Warmups start at 06:45 — one hour before the 07:45 puck drop. After three periods we expect you to have lost your voice.
